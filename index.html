Features · 主要功能

Built for the last rep.

Tools that exist for one reason — to keep you logging the next set, not fiddling with your phone. 為訓練而生的功能。沒有多餘的儀表板、沒有打字、沒有理由不記下這一組。

🎙

Voice Logging

Speak a set in Chinese or English. Zero typing between rounds. 語音記錄訓練組數，支援繁中與英文。組間零打字。

🤖

AI Parsed

Exercise, weight, reps and sets — auto-detected. Tap once to fix anything. 自動解析動作、重量、次數、組數，一鍵修正錯誤。

📅

Training Diary

Calendar view auto-tags every session — push, pull, legs at a glance. 月曆視圖按日期回顧每一次訓練，推拉腿一目了然。

📈

PR Autopilot

Automatic PR detection. Big-three strength trends update in real time. 個人最佳紀錄自動偵測，三大項力量趨勢圖即時更新。

⏱

Rest Timer

Built-in between-set rest timer. Keep your tempo locked in. 組間休息計時，讓節奏穩定可控。

🔒

Local Only

Fully offline. Your data stays on your device. No account, ever. 完全離線可用，資料只存在你的裝置上，無需註冊帳號。

How It Works · 三步驟

Three steps. No typing.

From last rep to logged set in one sentence. 從練完一組到日記完成，只需要一句話。

01

Tap Record

Press record. Hands stay on the bar. 按下錄音鍵，雙手不必離開槓鈴。

02

Say The Set

“Bench, 80 kilos, 8 reps, 3 sets.” That's it. 「臥推 80 公斤 8 下三組」自然講完。

03

Logged

AI writes the entry. PRs and trends update instantly. AI 自動寫入日記，PR 與趨勢圖即時更新。

JUST
SPEAK.

Stay in the lift — let your voice do the logging. 用講的，記下每一組。
