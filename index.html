RepLog

用講的，就能記錄每一組訓練 Log every set just by speaking.

舉完一組，按下錄音，自然地說出你做的動作——「臥推 80 公斤 8 下三組」—— RepLog 用 AI 把語音轉成完整的訓練紀錄，讓你專注在動作上，而不是手機上。

Finish a set, tap record, and say what you just did — “Bench press, 80 kilos, 8 reps, 3 sets.” RepLog turns your voice into a complete workout log, so you stay focused on the lift, not your phone.

主要功能 Features

  • 🎙️ 語音記錄訓練組數（支援繁中、英文） Voice logging for sets (Traditional Chinese & English).
  • 🤖 AI 自動解析動作、重量、次數、組數 AI auto-parses exercise, weight, reps and sets.
  • 📅 訓練日記，按日期回顧每一次訓練 Training diary — review every workout by date.
  • 📈 個人最佳紀錄（PR）自動偵測 + 力量趨勢圖 Automatic PR detection plus strength trend charts.
  • ⚖️ 體重變化追蹤 Bodyweight tracking over time.
  • ⏱️ 組間休息計時 Built-in rest timer between sets.
  • 🔒 完全離線可用，資料只存在你的裝置上 Fully offline — your data never leaves your device.
