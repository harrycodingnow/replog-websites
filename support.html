FAQ · 常見問題

Q: Voice recognition isn't accurate. What can I do?

Go to Settings → Language and make sure the right language is selected (English or Traditional Chinese). Try recording in a quieter space, and check that you've granted microphone and speech recognition permissions.

Q：語音辨識不準確怎麼辦？

請至「設定 → 語言」確認選擇了正確的語言（繁體中文或英文），並在安靜環境中錄音。也請確認系統已授予麥克風與語音辨識權限。

Q: Where is my data stored?

All workout data is stored locally on your iPhone using Apple's SwiftData. Nothing is synced to the cloud automatically. We recommend backing up your device with iCloud regularly.

Q：訓練紀錄儲存在哪裡？會不會不見？

所有資料都儲存在你的 iPhone 本機裝置上（使用 Apple 的 SwiftData）。資料不會自動同步到雲端，建議定期透過 iCloud 備份你的裝置。

Q: The AI got it wrong. How do I fix it?

Tap the parsed result to manually edit the exercise, weight, reps, or sets before saving.

Q：AI 解析錯誤怎麼辦？

點擊解析結果可以手動修正動作、重量、次數、組數任一欄位後再儲存。

Q: Can I export my workout data?

Export isn't available in 1.0, but it's on the roadmap.

Q：可以匯出我的訓練資料嗎？

1.0 版尚未支援匯出功能，已列入未來版本規劃。

Q: Do I need to pay or sign up?

No. RepLog is completely free and requires no account.

Q：我需要付費或註冊帳號嗎？

不用。RepLog 完全免費，也不需要註冊任何帳號。
